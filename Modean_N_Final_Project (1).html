Section 1: Business Questions to be Answered¶

We are posing as investors, looking to invest in one of the game systems in the market. We have narrowed our options to 2 of the biggest gaming systems on the market today, the PS4 and the XboxOne. We want to look at data to help decide which system is better to invest in right now. Some questions we will look to answer are:¶

Does one system appear in more positive or negative tweets than the other?¶

Based on monetary earnings so far, does one system outweigh the other?¶

Within the gaming community. does one system have a better reputation than the other?¶

Section 7: Storytelling¶

Going back to our original business questions, we wanted to look at which gaming system was better to invest in, the XboxOne or the PS4. We believe that based on the Twitter data collected from Twitter API, that there wasn't a real winner. The PS4 had more positive reviews collected, but also more negative reviews collected, partly due to more tweets being collected, but the ratio was fairly similar between the two systems. So based on Twitter API, both systems were pretty even. But when we look at the numbers we collected through webcrawling, we can see that the PS4 is well ahead of the XboxOne. When you compare the sales of both systems, the PS4 has more than doubled how many XboxOnes have been sold so far. And when you look at the gaming community, the PS4 comes out on top as well, albeit a little closer. The PS4 edged out the overall rating of the XboxOne on the websites of Engadget and CNet. So when we add all of our business answers togeth, we can solidly give the edge to the PS4 as the better system to invest in. People tweet about it more, they have over double the amount of sales, and are regarded as a better system overall in the gaming community than the Xbox One.
